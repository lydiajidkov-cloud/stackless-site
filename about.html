About Stackless

The Experiment

Stackless started as an experiment: what happens when someone with domain expertise uses AI tools to build real products, without a traditional development stack?

The result is a growing catalogue of practical tools and templates, spanning healthcare, personal finance, and consumer apps. Everything is built with AI development tools. No dev team. No framework dependencies. No stack. That's the name.

The Approach

Domain First

Every product starts with deep domain knowledge. Healthcare pathways built by a doctor. Finance tools designed by someone who actually uses them. AI handles the implementation, expertise drives the vision.

Real Products

These are not proof-of-concept projects. They are practical templates handling real data, solving real problems. Complete, documented, and ready to deploy.

No Lock-In

Every product ships with source files, documentation, and clear instructions. Edit the data, customise the logic, deploy it yourself. No vendor lock-in, no ongoing subscriptions.

Founder

I'm a doctor who ended up in health informatics and clinical AI. I now lead clinical strategy at Quadrivia (AI voice agents for healthcare) and build digital tools on the side using AI, without writing traditional code.

I share what works, what doesn't, and what I've learned along the way.

Mission

Domain experts should be able to build their own tools. AI makes that possible. I'm proving it by shipping real products.

This is not about replacing developers. It's about enabling people with expertise in healthcare, finance, education, and other domains to build the tools they need, when they need them.
