About Stackless

The Experiment

Stackless started as an experiment: what happens when someone who isn't a developer uses AI tools to build real products, without a traditional development stack?

The result is a growing catalogue of practical tools and templates, spanning personal finance, consumer apps, and gaming. Everything is built with AI development tools. No dev team. No framework dependencies. No stack. That's the name.

The Approach

Domain First

Every product starts with a real problem. Finance tools designed by someone who actually uses them. Consumer apps built because the existing ones weren't good enough. AI handles the implementation, curiosity drives the vision.

Real Products

These are not proof-of-concept projects. They are practical templates handling real data, solving real problems. Complete, documented, and ready to deploy.

No Lock-In

Every product ships with source files, documentation, and clear instructions. Edit the data, customise the logic, deploy it yourself. No vendor lock-in, no ongoing subscriptions.

Founder

I'm not a coder. No technical background. I'm the kind of person who built their own PC and had a Raspberry Pi but never actually got around to coding. I tried to learn a few times, it never stuck. AI development tools changed that.

I share what works, what doesn't, and what I've learned along the way.

Mission

You shouldn't need to be a developer to build your own tools. AI makes that possible. I'm proving it by shipping real products.

This is not about replacing developers. It's about enabling curious people to build the tools they need, when they need them, without waiting for someone else to build it for them.
