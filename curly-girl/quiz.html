Discover Your Hair Profile

Answer 7 quick questions to get a personalised CGM routine with UK product suggestions.

What's your hair texture?

Look at your hair after it air-dries without products. Most people have multiple types across their head, so pick the dominant one.

What's your hair porosity?

Porosity = how well your hair absorbs and retains moisture. Try the float test: place a clean dry strand in room-temperature water.

How dense is your hair?

Density = how many strands you have overall. Look at your scalp: can you see it easily through your hair?

What's your strand thickness?

Strand thickness (width) is different from density. Roll a single strand between your fingers.

How would you describe your scalp?

Think about how your scalp feels by day 2 or 3 after washing.

What's your main hair concern?

Pick the one that bothers you most. Your routine will prioritise this.

What's your budget preference?

All budgets can do CGM well. The best products aren't always the most expensive.
